IBM Bob – Request and access the free trial plan
Purpose
This document provides step-by-step instructions to request, activate, and access the IBM Bob free trial plan, including initial setup and account access.
Free trial plan Bobcoin limitation
Each interaction with Bob's AI capabilities consumes Bobcoins. The free trial plan comes with a limitation of 40 Bobcoins for 30 days. When Bobcoins are exhausted or the 30 days end, the account becomes inactive unless upgraded to a paid plan.
Step 1 - Subscribe for the trial plan
Open the Bob register for trial URL on your system browser.
Create an IBM account by filling the form and select Next button. If you already have an existing IBM account, you can proceed to login and complete the authentication.
Verify your email by entering the 7-digit code sent to your email and check the optional IBM keeping you informed on products and services. Select the Submit button.
An IBM and Bob account will be created and you will get a confirmation that your trial account is ready.
You will also receive an email confirmation for subscribing for the trial plan.
Step 2 – Get started with your free trial and access admin portal
After successfully subscribing for the trial plan, the Bob team will take about 5 minutes to get your account ready. Once it is ready, you will receive an email confirmation and then you can download and get started using Bob. 
Access your Bob admin portal to track your Bob usage and see account related information. 
Follow the below instructions to access your admin portal:
Login to IBM Bob account using your free trial subscription email.
Access the admin portal. If you have multiple Bob accounts, please ensure to select the right instance.
You will see your trial subscription plan details.
